Frequently Asked Questions

  • When is Wipe?

    Force Wipe: This will occur on the first Friday of each month at 4 AM AEST or 6 AM AEDT (depending on whether it is non-daylight savings or daylight savings). At times, this schedule can be delayed if Facepunch encounters a last-minute patch.

    Monthly Server: This server will wipe on force wipe as explained above.

    Wipe Command: You can use !wipe in our Discord to see when a server will wipe next.

  • There is an in-game glitch. Can you fix it?

    We do not interfere with the natural course of gameplay, nor do we provide compensation for any items lost due to unforeseeable glitches that may occur during gameplay.

    These glitches may include but are not limited to:

    • Falling through the world and losing your items.
    • Dropping loot on the ground and it disappearing or getting stuck.
    • Placing walls that cannot be removed/rotated after the timer expires.
    • Losing loot after a server rollback.
  • Do the admins or moderators ever play on the server?

    While our team is comprised of experienced and passionate individuals who possess extensive knowledge of the game, we have implemented a strict policy to avoid any potential conflict of interest or favoritism. As such, our administrators refrain from participating in gameplay activities, in order to maintain impartiality and ensure a fair and equitable experience for all our players. (Please note, admins aren't allowed to play; however, chat mods can.)

  • How do I report a player for cheating?

    Maintaining fair and enjoyable gameplay is of utmost importance to us. Therefore, in the event that a player is suspected of violating our rules or engaging in cheating, we encourage the community to report such incidents using our official reporting channels. This can be done through:

    • Rust's in-game F7 reporting system, which will immediately notify our administrators of any in-game reports. This report is also sent to Rusts EAC (Easy Anti Cheat) which may ban them for cheating.
    • Players can also submit a support ticket through our Discord server, ensuring that sufficient and concrete evidence is provided to substantiate any reports (screenshots, video proof etc.).

    We thank you for your cooperation in helping us to ensure a safe and fair gaming experience for all!

  • Do we really keep all our researched blueprints forever on the "No BP Wipe" server?

    It's up to the server owners to decide when to wipe blueprints. At Rustralia, we keep all blueprints.

  • Toxic Behaviour

    We take a strong stance against the use of hateful language, racism, and homophobia in our community and have implemented a protocol to ensure that all players adhere to this standard. As such, we may temporarily mute players who engage in the use of such language. Please note that all mutes have a specified duration and will be lifted automatically upon completion.

    However, we would like to emphasise that repeated instances of excessive toxicity, even after being muted, will not be tolerated. In such cases, we reserve the right to issue a toxic behavior ban, should it be deemed necessary. We value and prioritise the wellbeing of our community and strive to foster a positive and inclusive gaming environment.

  • I lost my items due to cheaters. Can I get my loot back?

    We cannot provide a refund for any lost gear or items. Even with images or clip evidence, it is difficult for us to ascertain the exact nature and extent of the loss experienced by every player. As such, we do not offer refunds for lost items, as it would be unfair to those who have experienced similar losses but are unable to provide the same level of evidence. However there can be some case by case scenarios.

  • I was banned. Can I appeal it?

    We understand that receiving a ban can be a frustrating experience, and we strive to ensure that all bans are issued fairly and with just cause. We provide an appeal system for players who wish to challenge a ban through our support ticket system on Discord. To submit an appeal, please navigate to https://rustralia.com.au/discord where you will be asked to verify your Steam and Discord accounts to confirm your identity.

    Once verified, please proceed to the support-ticket channel on our Discord server and select the ‘Ban or Mute Appeal’ option. We request that all necessary and accurate information is provided to facilitate the appeals process. Please note that it may take up to 48 hours for us to respond to your appeal.

Server Rules

  • Rule 1: Report any rule-breaking behaviour
    Report any rule-breaking behaviour using the in-game F7 report function or by creating a ticket in our discord.
  • Rule 2: Players are allowed one Rust game-banned alternative account
    Players are allowed one Rust game-banned alternative account that is over 150 days old. If a player is found with more than one alternative account that has a Rust game ban, they will be banned from the server. Players with 1 banned account can appeal this ban once it has been 150 days since the ban was issued.
  • Rule 3: The use of any third-party applications is prohibited
    The use of any third-party applications, scripts, macros, or other tools (not including crosshairs or guitar scripts) that give players an unfair advantage is prohibited. Any players found using such tools will be banned.
  • Rule 4: Discussing or promoting the use of cheats is not allowed
    Discussing or promoting the use of cheats, scripts, macros, or other tools that give players an unfair advantage is not allowed. Players who do so will receive a warning and may be banned if they continue to violate this rule. Repeated violations of this rule will result in a permanent ban.
  • Rule 5: Players are expected to treat each other with respect
    Players are expected to treat each other with respect. Any racist, sexually inappropriate, homophobic or otherwise offensive comments/hate speech will result in a mute or ban, depending on the severity of the offense.
  • Rule 6: Exploiting game bugs or glitches is prohibited
    Exploiting game bugs or glitches is prohibited. Depending on the severity of the exploit, players may be banned for doing so.
  • Rule 7: Do not release personal information
    Do not release personal information (including Steam and Discord profile pictures) of other players without their permission. We class this as a form of doxxing. Doing so is grounds for a permanent ban. Even if the information is public that does not mean you should use it.
  • Rule 8: Impersonating server staff is not allowed
    Impersonating server staff is not allowed. Players who violate this rule will be muted or banned, depending on the severity of the offence.
  • Rule 9: Threatening to DDOS the server or other players is strictly prohibited
    Threatening to DDOS the server or other players is strictly prohibited. Players who violate this rule will be permanently banned or muted.
  • Rule 10: The Rustralia staff reserve the right to make final decisions
    The Rustralia staff reserve the right to make final decisions on all matters related to the server.
  • Rule 11: Respect the staff
    The staff do not get paid, they do this out of the kindness of their hearts, so please treat them with respect.
  • Admin abuse
    If a player believes they have been the victim of admin abuse, they can contact the server at contactrustralia@gmail.com, only the owner will see these.

By playing on our servers you are consenting to our rules and understand the consequences for not following them
